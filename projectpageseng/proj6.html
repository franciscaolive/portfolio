Treasure Hunt

In collaboration with CTOE - Centro de Tropas de Operações Especiais

This project was developed for the course unit Multimédia II in 2024, in collaboration with Centro de Tropas de Operações Especiais de Lamego.

In this project, our mission was to develop innovative digital products that enrich and promote the museum experience of CTOE, to better transmit their historic and cultural elements, in its branches and military missions.

This project was developed in collaboration with my colleagues Alice Logvina and Ana Pinto. After visiting the CTOE space, we brainstormed and decided we'd create a rescue mission inside the museum.

Rangers are known for their rescue missions, so we decided to create a game with that theme for android. It's in a treasure hunt style, in which the player gets selected to rescue the leopard in CTOE's symbol. The player has to answer questions about the history of CTOE, and the answers are in the contents of the museum. The game includes a map with an exclamation point that indicates in which room of the museum the answer can be found.

This game was developed in Unity and the visual components were created in Procreate and Adobe Illustrator.
