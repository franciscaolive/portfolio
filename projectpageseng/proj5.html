Posters Ópera Arquivo Coliseu Ageas

In collaboration with FIATO - Festival Internacional de Artes e Ópera do Porto

This project was developed for the course unit Multimédia II, in 2024 in collaboration with Festival Internacional de Artes e Ópera do Porto.

It was proposed to us the creation of an Augmented Reality app, that brought the posters in the Festival to life. This project was developed in group, with my colleagues Alice Logvina and Ana Pinto. I took care of every visual 2D component, with Procreate and Adobe Illustrator. My colleagues developed the app in Unity and creatd the 3D elements.

We were in charge of developing this project for the operas Rigoletto and Turandot. For Rigoletto, we decided to create a 3D chess board, that would recreate a snippet of the opera in a small animation. In this opera, the main characters are the Court Jester, his daughter and the Duke of Mãntua. It's a tragic story that tells us about betrayal and vengeance. While trying to plan the murder of the Duke, the Jester hires a killer that ends up killing his daughter. That's the scene we decided to animate for this poster.

Turandot is an opera that tells the story of Princess Turandot. Her father is forcing her to find a man to marry, and so she prepares three riddles for her suitors, promising to get married to the one who got all three of them correctly. The ones that get them wrong, are killed. So, in this project, we decided to present to the public the riddles created by the princess in the style of a 2D quiz.
