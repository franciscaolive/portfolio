Survival Guide to College

Everything a student needs to know for a succesful uni experience!

1 / 3

This project was developed for my Design II course in 2022, on the first semester of my second year.

We were prompted to write a sarcastic book to offer guidance on how to survive college, design it, print it and assemble it. All illustrations were made by me on Procreate. The text was written by me and later added on Adobe InDesign.
