A Cidade do Futuro

In collaboration with Ocubo e a Alfândega do Porto.

This project was developed for the course unit Multimédia in collaboration with Ocubo. This project's goal was to explore in a conceptual manner, our visions of a futuristic city. Through 2D and 3D animaitons, mixing the real and the digital, the intention is to take the spectators to a journey that explores the limit of the Human evolution.
