Ciclo Obscuro

The last adventure of two friends...

This project was developed for the course unit of Animação III, where we brought the story written by our colleague Alice Logvina to life. We modeled the characters and the sets in Autodesk Maya, recorded the movements and created the animations in Autodesk Motion Builder. In this project, I was responsible for modeling my character Verity, and the exterior of the house in which this story is set in. Besides that, I also created the animation Clips of my character, her facial expressions and her hand movements. I also assembled and rendered every part of the animation set on the outside and in the living room.

This project ended up not coming out exactly how we wanted it to, mainly due to time restrictions. Regardless, it taught me a lot about how Maya and Motion Builder work. By the end, I felt very confident about my understanding of these softwares.
