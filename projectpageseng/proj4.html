Ímpeto de Voar

Freedom is hard to define. Ímpeto de Voar tries to illustrate the moment we feel on top of the world, with no concerns or problems, feeling the adrenaline in our veins. The absense of any external coercion, the ability to act without fear.

Project developed on my senior year of High School. All drawings were made frame by frame on Procreate. Sound produced in Audacity utilizing the sound library freesound and edited on Adobe Premiere.

At the beggining of its conception, it was simply a movie about gymnastics, but the more I worked on it, the clearer it became it obtained a bigger meaning along the way.

This film won second place in ESEQ's Cinema Contest 8 E Meio.
