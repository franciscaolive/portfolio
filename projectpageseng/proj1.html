Afterglow

Afterglow is a project under development as part of the Project Course Unit, taught in the final semester of the Multimedia Bachelor's degree at ESMAD – School of Media Arts and Design of IPP – Polytechnic Institute of Porto.

This project aims to create a relaxing lifestyle game, where the player can explore a different world and find various objects.

It is still in the production phase.
