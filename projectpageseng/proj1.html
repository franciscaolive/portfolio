the fifth season

Road to Fashion Week.

the fifth season is a 2D game developed in Godot that immerses players in the world of fashion, allowing them to experience Fashion Week. Players take on the role of aspiring fashion icons, facing creative and social challenges while shaping their rise in the industry. The editorial and sophisticated aesthetic blends with an engaging narrative, offering a dynamic experience where players’ choices influence their character’s development and journey.

Combining fashion, strategy, and personalization, the game allows players to choose one of the four fashion capitals and participate in social and creative events that determine their success. The goal is to create an immersive experience accessible to a young audience, showcasing fashion as a form of personal expression. The Fifth Season aims to innovate in the dress-up game genre by offering a deep narrative with multiple endings based on player decisions.

This is the final project of my Bachelor's degree and it's currently being developed.
